How can we help you?

Find answers to frequently asked questions below or reach out to our team.

📦 How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days. Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day.
🔄 What is your return policy?
We offer a 14-Day Easy Returns policy on all items (except final sale items and certain beauty products). Items must be unworn and have original tags attached.
💳 What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and select digital wallets.
